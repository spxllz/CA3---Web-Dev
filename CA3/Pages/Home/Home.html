North Berwick, where coastal charm meets historic beauty

Founded: 1250

Location and Setting

Nestled along the captivating coastline of East Lothian, Scotland, North Berwick is a charming seaside town located approximately 25 miles east of Edinburgh. Its idyllic location offers breathtaking views of the North Sea and easy access to stunning sandy beaches, including the renowned West Beach and Milsey Bay.

Outdoor Activities

For outdoor enthusiasts, North Berwick provides a playground of adventure. From sailing and surfing to golfing on world-famous courses like North Berwick Golf Club, there's no shortage of activities to enjoy amidst the natural beauty of this coastal gem.

Historical Landmarks

Immerse yourself in North Berwick's rich history by exploring its fascinating landmarks. Discover the ancient ruins of Tantallon Castle, dating back to the 12th century, or marvel at the mystical Bass Rock, a volcanic island steeped in folklore and home to diverse seabird colonies.

Community and Culture

Experience the warmth and vibrancy of North Berwick's community. Delight in the local arts scene, indulge in fresh seafood at charming cafes and restaurants, and browse boutique shops offering unique gifts and souvenirs.

Whether you seek adventure, cultural experiences, or simply a peaceful retreat by the sea, North Berwick invites you to explore its scenic beauty and discover the magic of this coastal treasure.
