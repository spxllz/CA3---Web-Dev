North Berwick, where coastal charm meets historic beauty

Founded c.1250

Location and Setting

Nestled along the stunning coastline of East Lothian, Scotland, North Berwick is a charming seaside town located just 25 miles east of Edinburgh. Its idyllic location offers breathtaking views of the North Sea and easy access to stunning sandy beaches, including its very own West Beach and Milsey Bay.

Outdoor Activities

For outdoor enthusiasts, North Berwick provides a plenty of opportunities. From water-sports such as sailing and surfing, to skating at the new community funded skatepark, to golfing on world-famous courses like North Berwick Golf Club's West Links, there's no shortage of activities to enjoy with plenty of fun to be had during your visit.

Historical Landmarks

Immerse yourself in North Berwick's rich history by exploring its fascinating landmarks. Discover the ancient ruins of Tantallon Castle, dating back to the 12th century, or marvel at the mystical Bass Rock, a volcanic island steeped in folklore and home to diverse seabird colonies. There is plenty to be seen, all just a stone's throw away from the town centre.

Community and Culture

Experience the warmth and vibrancy of North Berwick's community. Check out in the local arts scene, indulge in fresh seafood at charming cafes and restaurants such as the Herringbone or The Rocketeer, and browse boutique shops offering unique gifts and souvenirs that can only be found locally on our high street.

Whether you seek adventure, cultural experiences, or simply a peaceful retreat by the sea, North Berwick invites you to explore its scenic beauty and discover the magic of this coastal treasure.
